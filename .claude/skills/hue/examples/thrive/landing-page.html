what it is

a quiet place to note how the week actually went, without turning it into a game.

thrive is built around one idea: the most useful thing a tracker can do is stay out of your way. no badges, no rewards, no notifications that nudge. you log what happened, thrive keeps the record, and over time small patterns become visible on their own.
